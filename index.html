What is the future of IoT?

The coolest thing about IoT might be its limitless possibilities. Rapid growth in related technologies continues to fuel more and more use cases.Considered futuristic just a few years ago, self-driving cars are on the verge of becoming mainstream. Lower carbon emissions, intelligent roadways, and renewable energy, in part facilitated by IoT, will help to make the planet more sustainable and our lives richer.Advances in networking, security, analytics, and cloud computing are making a smarter, more connected future possible for all of us.
